Aayansh


Aayansh, organized by NSS MMMUT Gorakhpur, was a beautiful reminder of the power of giving this Diwali. Students and faculty came together to donate stationery, offering more than just supplies-they gave the gift of possibility to the underprivileged. What started as a simple act of kindness became something truly meaningful. Aayansh perfectly captured the essence of Diwali.

Through this gesture, Aayansh reflects the power of unity and compassion. The initiative brought smiles to young learners and reinforced that education is the foundation of a brighter future.

Blood Donation Camp


Team NSS MMMUT Gorakhpur feels proud to announce the successful organization of a blood donation camp through the collaborative efforts of Team NSS MMMUT and BRD Medical College. We extend our sincerest gratitude to the selfless service of individuals who generously donated their blood, becoming the beacon of hope for others in their time of need.

This blood donation camp had a very successful impact on society's health. Team NSS feels very thankful and heartfelt to all the donors who support saving others' lives.
